### 美國 / United States

**合理使用 (17 U.S.C. § 107)**

本項目符合合理使用嘅四個因素：

1. **使用目的及性質**：非商業、具轉化性嘅研究及新聞報導用途
2. **版權作品嘅性質**：主要為事實性新聞報導
3. **使用部分嘅比例**：僅存檔必要部分以保存證據
4. **對市場嘅影響**：無商業競爭；保存可能消失嘅公共利益材料

**Fair Use (17 U.S.C. § 107)**

This project satisfies the four fair use factors:

1. **Purpose and character of use**: Non-commercial, transformative research and news reporting
2. **Nature of copyrighted work**: Primarily factual news reporting
3. **Amount used**: Only what is necessary to preserve evidence
4. **Effect on market**: No commercial competition; preserving materials of public interest that may disappear

### 英國 / United Kingdom

**《1988年版權、設計及專利法》例外條款**

- **第29條**：為研究及私人學習目的嘅公平處理
- **第30(1)條**：為批評、評論及新聞報導目的嘅公平處理
- **第30(2)條**：為報導時事目的嘅公平處理

**Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 Exceptions**

- **Section 29**: Fair dealing for research and private study
- **Section 30(1)**: Fair dealing for criticism, review, and news reporting
- **Section 30(2)**: Fair dealing for reporting current events

### 香港 / Hong Kong

**《版權條例》（第528章）例外條款**

- **第38條**：為研究或私人研習目的嘅公平處理
- **第39條**：為批評、評論及新聯報導目的嘅公平處理
- **第40條**：為報導時事目的嘅公平處理

**Copyright Ordinance (Cap. 528) Exceptions**

- **Section 38**: Fair dealing for research or private study
- **Section 39**: Fair dealing for criticism, review, and news reporting
- **Section 40**: Fair dealing for reporting current events
